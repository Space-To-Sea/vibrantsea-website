Ocean Phytoplankton:

TIMELAPSE

hide cloudy dates

The NASA Landsat 8 satellite orbits over Stellwagen Bank every 16 days. In this grid format, we are able to scroll to see the changes in phytoplankton concentrations throughout the years. As you will see, a major obstacle of satellite image processing is cloud coverage. Click the button to the left to show or hide days with total cloud cover.
